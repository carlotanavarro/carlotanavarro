About Me



I have vast experience working and interacting with different cultures and nationalities, having worked in several international companies such as the Walt Disney Company, TUI Hotels or more recently in Grupo Gourmets collaborating in the management and organization of Salón Gourmets. These experiences have helped me gain the ability to pick up on new skills such as marketing and communications, customer relations, time management, activity planning and team motivation to name a few, as well as improve my English and gain a basic understanding of Italian and Mandarin.

I'm a very outgoing and dynamic person and enjoy working with people from all parts of the world. I believe in the importance of communication and respect, not only towards the clients, but also with my team members in order to maintain a strong team morale. I am passionate about the work I do, I’m an incredibly hard worker, and I always keep a positive attitude, and these attributes are why I believe I would be a great asset to your team.

Salón Gourmets

Once again, this past April, 2023, we held a new edition of Salón Gourmets, breaking records in numbers and meeting our desired objective of becoming a mirror and showcase for Spanish gastronomy, as well as, in my professional endeavor, promoting and bringing the Spain brand to every corner of the world. If someone asks me which is the secret of success in an event, after my still brief but intense experience, my answer would still be a good team. A good synergy in the team, communication and willingness to always support each other is key to obtain successful results.
I have had the good fortune to lead a team of professionals whose willingness and motivation I could not be prouder to boast. There are many stressful situations while running events and it is important to effectively manage every crisis that may arise. This requires that each member exudes confidence and a positive attitude, and it is up to the leader to promote and set an example of it. Again, thank you to each of my team members and congratulations for being an example of how teamwork is key to succeed.
